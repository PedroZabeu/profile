# EPIC 4 Brainstorm

## Adventures Page

This brainstorm has the objective of defining a plan for the Adventures Page, which will be the root (/adventures) experience. We will explore a few different options  (I will give the main ideas and AI will structure them) for the page, and then we will define the final plan.

## Options

- **Option 1:** Map in the Fullscreen
- **Option 2:** Map in a container on the right with a sidebar on the left
- **Option 3:** Sidebar in the left, globe in the right

## Option 1: Map in the Fullscreen

This option with have a dotted world map in the fullscreen, with dots highlighting the adventure sites. When the user clicks on a dot, it will open a modal with the adventure details. The modal will have a close button to close it.

### Option 2: Map in a container on the right with a sidebar on the left

This option with have a dotted world map in a container on the right, with a sidebar on the left. The sidebar will have a list of the adventure sites, and when the user clicks on a site, it will open a modal with the adventure details. The modal will have a close button to close it. there will be an animation highlighting the location of the adventure site on the map before opening the modal.

### Option 3: Sidebar in the left, globe in the right

This option with have a dotted world map in a container on the right, with a sidebar on the left. The sidebar will have a list of the adventure sites, and when the user clicks on a site, it will open a modal with the adventure details. The modal will have a close button to close it. there will be an animation highlighting the location of the adventure site on the map before opening the modal. This animation will rotate the globe to the location of the adventure site.
