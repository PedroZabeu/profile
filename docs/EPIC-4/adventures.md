- **Location**: Wyoming
- **Activity**: Backcountry Snowboarding
- **Date**: 2013
- **Description**: Hiked on foot mountains on the Teton Pass, Wyoming and rode down on my snowboard. Mount Glory was the main attraction. I went by myself, with no prior experience in the mountain. 

## 2. Patagonia - Trekking

- **Location**: Torres del Paine, El Chalten, El Calafate
- **Activity**: Trekking
- **Date**: 2016, 2018
- **Description**: Trekking and camping in the mountains of Patagonia. Went with my mother and daughter and we hiked the W Trek in Torres del Paine and the Laguna de los Tres and Cerro Torre treks in El Chalten. Total distance hiked exceeded 100km.

## 3. Bolivia - Huayna Potosi

- **Location**: Huayna Potosi, Bolivia
- **Activity**: Mountaineering
- **Date**: 2022
- **Description**: We did an extensive preparation in the Condoriri Massif, Bolivia, to acclimatize and train for the summit of Huayna Potosi. We hiked and camped in the mountains for several days, reaching altitudes of over 5000m. Then we headed to Huayna Potosi and summited it in a 4 day expedition, which included crevasse rescue training, ice climbing and glacier travel. We spent a night in high camp at 5500m before heading to the summit at 6088m. I started dating my wife after we reached the summit!

## 4. Bolivia - Road of Death

- **Location**: Bolivia
- **Activity**: Mountain Biking
- **Date**: 2022
- **Description**: Mountain biking down the North Yungas Road, also known as the "Road of Death". It is a 64 km long road with a vertical drop of 3,635 meters. It is considered one of the most dangerous roads in the world. I did this with my wife and we had a blast!

## 6. Red Rocks - Rock Climbing

- **Location**: Red Rocks, Nevada
- **Activity**: Rock Climbing
- **Date**: 2023
- **Description**: Rock climbing in Red Rocks National Conservation Area. I went with my wife and we climbed Armatron, a 5.10a trad route. We went in the winter and got lost after nightfall. The whole expecience took more than 20 hours of non-stop climbing and hiking. In the end, we managed to get back to the car safely. It really was a true adventure!

## 7. Mallorca - Deep Water Solo

- **Location**: Mallorca, Spain
- **Activity**: Deep Water Solo
- **Date**: 2021
- **Description**: Deep water soloing in Mallorca, Spain. I went by myself and climbed 20 meters high routes above the Mediterranean sea. It was summertime, the water was warm and the sun was shining. I climbed for hours and had a great time. It was kind of scary to climb without a rope, but it was also thrilling and exciting. After a few falls, I got used to it and really enjoyed the experience.
