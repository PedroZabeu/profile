## üß± Key Components

- **Location**: `components/animated-showcase/sections/ContactDetailTooltip.tsx`
- **Design Specifications**:
    - **Background**: Alpine Mist `#F1F5F9` (Light, crisp background)
    - **Outline/Border**: Slate Rock `#4B5866` (Subtle gray-blue border)
    - **Typography**: 
        - Font: **Comfortaa**
        - Color: Night Summit (Navy Blue) `#0D1B2A`
- **Features**:
    - **Detail Reveal**: Shows the actual email, handle, or number.
    - **Copy Action**: Clicking the bubble/box copies the content to the clipboard.
    - **Visual Feedback**: Transition to a "Copied!" state for 2 seconds.

### 2. Update `ShowcaseDetails`
- **Strict Requirement**: DO NOT change the existing icon designs or their animations.
- Integrate the `ContactDetailTooltip` as an overlay or proximity reveal.
