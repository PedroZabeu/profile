## üõ∞Ô∏è Technical Implementation Details

1.  **Label Positioning Logic:**
    *   Left Column Width: ~25% (Fixed/Sticky).
    *   Center Column: Overflow-X: Hidden (Controlled by JS scroll sync).
    *   Right Column Width: ~15% (Fixed/Sticky).
2.  **Synchronized Scrolling (The "Follow Path" Hook):**
    *   As the user scrolls the `CareerBox` vertically, the center pane's `scrollLeft` must be adjusted to keep the active milestone dot within the center 50% of the visible viewport.
3.  **Expedition Navigation Controls:**
    *   **Jump-to-Basecamp:** A button to instantly scroll to the bottom (Earliest milestone).
    *   **Jump-to-Summit:** A button to instantly scroll to the top (Future/Goal).
    *   Location: Positioned in the `CareerBox` header or as a sticky overlay at the bottom-right of the log.
4.  **Visual Overlap Protection:**
    *   Labels are right-aligned *within* the left sticky column.
    *   Year markers are left-aligned *within* the right sticky column.
    *   Font sizes: `Company/Title: 11px`, `Sub-Role: 9px`. All Oxanium.
5.  **Milestone Marker (Dot):**
    *   Standard dots: 3px radius, Glacier Steel.
    *   Current (Z-Score): 5px radius, Alpine Blue pulse effect.
