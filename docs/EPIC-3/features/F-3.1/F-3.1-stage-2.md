# Feature Plan: F-3.1 Stage 2 - Log Entry Architecture

This stage implements the core content structure of the Career Log, focusing on the milestone level lines, vertical scrolling, and the "Information Pinning" behavior (Sticky Labels).

---

---

---

## üèóÔ∏è Visual & Technical Objective

- **Log Entries:** Zero-description minimal log. Focused on identity and chronology.
- **Fixed Geometry:** The `CareerBox` dimensions are absolute. Content MUST be contained within its current layout; all overflow is handled by internal **vertical and horizontal** scrolling.
- **Bi-Directional Scrolling:** 
    - **Vertical:** Standard list progression with the "Scissoring" effect for milestones.
    - **Horizontal:** Allows for ultra-wide text or future data columns without expanding the physical box. Sticky labels (Company/Period) remain pinned to the viewport edges during lateral movement.
- **The "High-Horizon" Line:** A solid horizontal separator (`border-glacier-steel/20`) that divides identity labels.
- **Internal Padding:** Entries maintain a strict margin from the box borders. Lines and text MUST NOT touch the edges of the `CareerBox`.
- **Minimalist Aesthetic:** 
    - No boxes or borders around text elements. 
    - Backgrounds are transparent.
    - Only text and the horizontal "Horizon Line" are visible.
- **Layout Construction:**
    - **Header Row (Sticky Horizontal):** 
        - **Left:** Company Name (`sticky left-0`).
        - **Right:** Period/Year range (`sticky right-0`).
    - **Separator Row:** The full-width Horizon Line (constrained by padding).
    - **Sub-Header Row:** Position Title below the line (anchored left).
- **Vertical "Scissoring":** Each professional milestone is configured as a `sticky top-0` block. As you scroll, the current entry remains "pinned" at the top of the `CareerBox` until the next milestone pushes it out of the viewport.
